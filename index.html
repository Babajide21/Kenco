what is home watch?

Do you spend winters in Southeast Florida and summers at a second home? Do you take extended vacations throughout the year? If so, Home Watch services offer peace of mind that your home receives care while you're away.

What Is Home Watch Service?

Home Watch service provides regular inspections of a property while the home is unoccupied by the owner. Checks typically occur weekly at alternating times to investigate the property and address any issues.

The Home Watch aims to prevent unwanted damage and maintain the home's essential functions. In addition, simple tasks like removing flyers or mail create the impression that the property is occupied.

With the Home Watch service, the homeowner receives updates and has a point of contact to allow third parties to enter the home, like home service providers or contractors.

Home Watch offers regular and predictable service while you're away so you don't have to manage the details of your home or rely on neighbors.

What Are the Benefits of Home Watch?

For snowbirds and other long-term travelers, Home Watch provides many benefits. Even with smart home technology, like doorbell cameras and thermostats, you still need someone who can manage property issues in your absence.

Home Watch Benefits

Decrease Risk of Property Damage - If a problem occurs, like a water leak, mold growth, or pest infestation, the Home Watch professional alerts you of the problem. They assist with implementing a quick solution to minimize damage.

Deter Intruders â€“ An empty home invites intruders and even squatters. Home Watch includes regular checks of the property. They remove mail, flyers, and other signs of vacancy.

Communicate with the Homeowner â€“ Home Watch service includes weekly updates. It also gives homeowners a point of contact for issues related to the home. For example, the Home Watch professional can provide entry and ensure task completion if you need to schedule HVAC maintenance.

Ensure Lawn Care Service Maintained â€“ Poor lawn care in a friendly neighborhood signal the owner isn't home. You'll know if your landscaping or lawn care service provider abandons the job or slacks off.

Implement Hurricane Storm Plan â€“ In Southeast Florida, hurricane season starts June 1st to November 30th. However, you don't want to worry about storm preparations while away. Home Watch service ensures your home safely. They also inspect the property after the storm to check for obvious damage.

Why Do You Need a Home Watch?

Home Watch protects your investment. Even minor damage that goes unaddressed can lead to significant problems. Consider how a small water leak can lead to extensive mold growth and water damage over several months.

Also, while most contractors act professionally, problems do occur. Home Watch service allows entry for service providers and inspects to ensure that the work has been completed and the home has been secured.

The bottom line, Home Watch keeps tabs on your home. You don't have to spend your time worrying about broken windows, overgrown grass, or a broken HVAC unit. Get peace of mind with Home Watch.
